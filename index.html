your music has a story to tell.

Every song you love came with a moment. A road trip. A heartbreak. A first time hearing it live. Reso is the place to save that story -- not a rating, not a playlist, but the real thing. Try it now and lmk if you would like to use it.

media-first cards
your photos and videos become the memory.

Drop a photo from that night. The concert ticket. The road trip screenshot. The stadium pic with your friends. Reso wraps it in the song that was playing and gives you space to write the story only you know.

Photos and videos from your camera roll sit alongside album art
Lyric cards pull the exact line that hit you -- your annotation lives below it
Per-card privacy -- this one's public, this one's friends-only, this one's just for you
